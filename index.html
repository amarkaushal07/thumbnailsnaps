YouTube Thumbnail Resolutions Explained

  • Ultra High (1280×720 – maxresdefault): Highest available HD resolution.
  • High Quality (640×480 – sddefault): Standard HD preview.
  • Medium Quality (480×360 – hqdefault): Common resolution.
  • Standard (320×180 – mqdefault): Smaller preview size.
  • Low Quality (120×90 – default): Minimal resolution thumbnail.

Can You Download YouTube Thumbnail in 4K?

YouTube typically does not generate 4K thumbnails. The maximum resolution available for most videos is 1280×720 pixels (HD). If a video does not provide a maximum resolution thumbnail, our tool automatically falls back to the next available quality.
