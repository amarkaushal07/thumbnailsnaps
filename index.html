YouTube Thumbnail Resolutions Explained

  • Ultra High (1280x720 – maxresdefault): Highest available HD resolution.
  • High Quality (640x480 – sddefault): Standard HD preview.
  • Medium Quality (480x360 – hqdefault): Common resolution.
  • Standard (320x180 – mqdefault): Smaller preview size.
  • Low Quality (120x90 – default): Minimal resolution thumbnail.

Can You Download YouTube Thumbnail in 4K?

YouTube does not typically generate 4K thumbnails. The maximum resolution available for most videos is 1280x720 pixels (HD). If a video does not provide a max resolution thumbnail, our tool automatically falls back to the next available quality.
