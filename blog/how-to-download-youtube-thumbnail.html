Method 2: Manual URL Trick

You can also manually access thumbnails using YouTube’s image servers. Replace VIDEO_ID with the video’s ID: 

https://img.youtube.com/vi/VIDEO_ID/maxresdefault.jpg

Available formats:

  • maxresdefault.jpg (Highest resolution)
  • sddefault.jpg
  • hqdefault.jpg
  • mqdefault.jpg
  • default.jpg

Understanding Thumbnail Resolutions

Most YouTube thumbnails are available in 1280×720 (HD). Not all videos provide max resolution thumbnails. If maxresdefault is unavailable, YouTube falls back to lower resolutions.

Can You Download YouTube Thumbnails in 4K?

YouTube does not typically generate 4K thumbnails. The maximum resolution usually available is 1280×720 pixels.
